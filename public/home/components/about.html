From a dream to a mission.

Foxxy was created as a goal to make it easier for people to get various things in one place at a low price.

How we started

Foxxy is a digital online shop established in Indonesia in 2021. After the founder, Rafly Maulana, found that the market demand to products like streaming account, game voucher, etc. is high, he decided to start Foxxy as his first project, as well as a bussiness.

But, over time, we understand how our potential buyers like products at low prices. Therefore, we started working with other traders to get products at low prices with ⎯ of course ⎯ good quality.

Not to forget, we also expand the variety of our sales products so that our prospective buyers will very easily get the product they want in one place.

Our Missions

See our target in the future to give you better experience at our services.

Cheap Price

We know that price is the number one problem when we want to buy a product. Therefore, here we strive to provide every product we have at low prices but with the best quality.

High Quality Customer Service

When we have received our purchased product, we often receive various kinds of errors in the product. Therefore we will continue to present the best help center in order to provide the best service to each of our customers.

Large Scale of Products

There is no need to explore various kinds of stores to get various kinds of products and compare to get the cheapest prices. Here we will continue to increase the variant of our products.

Large Scale of Customers

We also want to meet a wide variety of customers from different backgrounds so that we can enlarge our sales experience and improve the quality of our sales.
